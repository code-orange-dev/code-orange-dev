## April 2026

- **Bitcoin Dojo Cohort 1 graduated** — 21 graduates from 49 registrants. Partnership with [Chaincode Labs](https://chaincode.com/) BOSS Challenge. Graduates now pursuing Good First Issues across Bitcoin Core, rust-bitcoin, and BDK.
- **rawBit Study Cohort launched** — 10-week program building raw transactions from scratch with [@rawBit_io](https://x.com/rawBit_io). Covers Scripts, SegWit, multisig, timelocks with an interactive visual tool.
- **OpenClaw workshops started** — Monthly in-person sessions at Bitcoin House Bali covering AI-assisted custody with Nunchuk, Lightning L402 payments, Nostr website building with Soapbox Shakespeare, and Nostr bot zapping with Alby.
- **Impact Report published** — Full documentation of developer outcomes, PR contributions, workshop reach, and community growth. [View on GitHub →](https://github.com/code-orange-dev/impact-report)
- **PR Tracking Dashboard launched** — Monthly-updated tracking of every PR by Code Orange community members. [View on GitHub →](https://github.com/code-orange-dev/PR-tracking-dashboard)
- **Curriculum repo published** — Full CC0-licensed curriculum for all 6 programs with week-by-week syllabi, exercises, and facilitator guides. [View on GitHub →](https://github.com/code-orange-dev/curriculum)
- **Keypleb confirmed as speaker at [Bitcoin Asia 2026](https://asia.b.tc/agenda)** (Hong Kong)
- Upcoming: Nunchuk self-custody workshop — 22nd May, 5pm, Bitcoin House Bali

## March 2026

- **Bitcoin Dojo Week 5 completed** — Cohort studying how Bitcoin turns keys into addresses: compressed vs uncompressed SEC, SHA256 + RIPEMD160 hashing, Base58 vs Bech32 addresses. BOSS Challenge participants staying consistent.
- **Advanced Self-Custody Workshop** — Covered single-sig with SeedSigner, passphrase with Trezor, and multisig with Nunchuk. "So we could sleep well at night with our self-custody setup."
- **Fedimint workshop** — Monthly workshop with Fedi Community Master of SEA covering private messaging, shared custody, and mini apps on Fedimint federations.
- **Vibe Coding meets Bitcoin Dojo** — Community members vibe-coding websites to answer weekly Dojo questions. [@Chaitika_](https://x.com/Chaitika_) built a website teaching how Bitcoin validates a transaction. [@mwihotii](https://x.com/mwihotii) built another.

- Monthly privacy/self-custody/censorship-resistance workshop held
